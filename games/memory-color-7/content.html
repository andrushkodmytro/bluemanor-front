Welcome to Memory Colors!

When you're ready, hit Start to see the colors that make up the circle. Look at these and memorize them until the timer reaches zero.

Then, drag and drop the correct color from beside the circle into each section. When you're ready to check, hit Submit. After you get three rounds right, you'll move up a level! If you make a mistake in your circle, you will lose a life. After you lose three lives, the game ends.
